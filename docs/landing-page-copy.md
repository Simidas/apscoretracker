## 7. FAQ

**Q1: Is AP Score Tracker free?**
**A1:** Yes. The MVP is completely free with unlimited tracking, charts, and exports. We may add a Pro version later with PDF reports and study plans, but the core tracker will always be free.

---

**Q2: Do I need to create an account?**
**A2:** No. We don't have accounts, logins, or passwords. Your data is stored locally in your browser. When you come back, your scores are still there — as long as you haven't cleared your browser data.

---

**Q3: How accurate is the score prediction?**
**A3:** We use publicly available scoring curves based on College Board exam data. Predictions are typically within 0.5 points of official scores, but your actual AP score may vary. This tool is for tracking progress, not guaranteeing results.

---

**Q4: Which AP subjects are supported?**
**A4:** At launch: AP Lang, AP Psych, AP Calc AB, AP Calc BC, AP Physics 1, AP Physics C, AP Bio, AP Chem, AP Stats, AP US History. More subjects added based on demand.

---

**Q5: Can I export my data?**
**A5:** Yes. Click "Export JSON" to download a backup of all your practice test records. You can re-import it later if you switch browsers or clear your cache.

---

**Q6: Is my data private?**
**A6:** Completely. Your scores never leave your device. We don't have a server that stores your data — everything lives in your browser's localStorage. We can't see it, and neither can anyone else.

---

**Q7: Is this an official College Board tool?**
**A7:** No. AP Score Tracker is an independent tool built by a student developer. "AP" is a registered trademark of College Board. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or approved by College Board.

---

**Q8: What if I lose my data?**
**A8:** Since data is stored locally, clearing your browser cache will erase it. We recommend exporting a JSON backup regularly — especially before exam season.

---

**Q9: Does it work on mobile?**
**A9:** Yes. The site is fully responsive. You can enter scores and view charts on your phone between classes or during study breaks.

---

**Q10: How is this different from APScore5 or Albert.io?**
**A10:** APScore5 and Albert.io are comprehensive study platforms with practice questions and videos. We're a focused tracker — we don't replace your study materials, we help you measure if they're working.

## 8-Rule Self Check

### 1. Write for scanners ✅
- [x] 每个 section 有标题
- [x] 关键数字和结论突出（"within 0.5 points", "10+ subjects", "$0 forever"）
- [x] 用 bullet point，不用大段落
- [x] 段落不超过 3 行

### 2. One sentence, one idea ✅
- [x] "Choose your subject, enter your scores, and watch your progress curve grow." — 3 个短句，每句一个动作
- [x] 没有长复合句

### 3. You > We ✅
- [x] "You see a clear trend line" / "Your scores are private" / "You know exactly where to stand"
- [x] "We" 仅出现在必要说明（"We don't have accounts", "We use publicly available curves"）
- [x] You/Your 出现频率 > We/Our 频率

### 4. Specific > vague ✅
- [x] "within 0.5 points" 而不是 "very accurate"
- [x] "10+ AP subjects" 而不是 "many subjects"
- [x] "30 seconds" 而不是 "fast"
- [x] "$4.99/month" 而不是 "affordable"

### 5. Benefit > Feature ✅
- [x] "You know at a glance whether you're on track for a 5" (Benefit) > "Line chart showing predicted score" (Feature)
- [x] "Stop wasting time on chapters you've already mastered" (Benefit) > "Color-coded grid" (Feature)
- [x] 每个 Feature 都问了 "所以呢？"

### 6. CTA = Verb + Result ✅
- [x] "Start Tracking Free — No Signup Needed" (Start + Tracking)
- [x] "Track My First Practice Test — Free" (Track + Practice Test)
- [x] "See How It Works" (See + How It Works)
- [x] 没有 Submit / Learn More / Buy Now

### 7. Cut the first draft by 30% ✅
- [x] 删除了冗余形容词（"powerful", "advanced", "next-generation"）
- [x] 删除了重复说明（localStorage 解释只出现一次）
- [x] 每段都可删除而不损失核心信息

### 8. Read it out loud ✅
- [x] "Track Your AP Progress Across Every Practice Test" — 顺口
- [x] "See your score trends, spot weak topics, and know exactly what to study next" — 节奏感好
- [x] 没有卡壳或觉得假的地方
